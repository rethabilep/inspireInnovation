Approach

It is our belief that organisations ahould not solely rely on consultants and that consultants should only be needed on the short term or in extra ordinary circumstances

Vision

To be a leader in providing innovative business solution to entrepreneurs in Africa

Mission

To partner with our clients in solving business problems by providing consultancy, research & insights and training solutions

Services


Our consulting services comprise of business consulting and economic advisory services. Our aim is to help organisations to solve their biggest challenges and to realise their growth potential. We partner with our clients to give them access to our network of highly skilled professionals and we offer some of the following services:

  • Accounting and Tax Services
  • Business proposals and financial models
  • Systems and processes
  • Corporate Governance
  • Risk Management
  • Product Development
  • Digital Transformation
  • Data Collection and Analytics
  • Strategy

We have combined our technology (learning platform), our content, our partnerships and our expertise to form Inspire Learning Solutions to achieve one of our goals of capacitating individuals and organizations with the skills to solve complex business problems. We do this through two distinct offerings:

Inspire Learning Platform

Client-specific solutions

We design custom learning content based on our client's specific needs and we deliver this content through our learning facilitators and we provide clients an opportunity to make this content available in their organisation through our learning platform. Clients also have the option of utilizing our platform to host content that they have developed themselves

We partner with leading learning providers internationally to bring our clients skills development and training in professional (core skills) and technical skills. We deliver these services through a blended learning model (face-to-face and online). Our goal is to provide a learning platform on which our clients can access business related learning content

Through our research and insights service-offering, we aim to contribute to knowledge and understanding of Africa's entrepreneurship, technology and innovation landscape. Our research is aimed at providing in-depth knowlege on that can enable our clients to navigate how to do business in Africa. We also do client-commissioned research and feasibility studies based on client-specific needs. Some of our offerings are as follows:

  • Research publications
  • Feasibility studies
  • Market research and insights

About Likeleli Monyamane

I am a qualified Chartered Accountant registered with the South African Institute of Chartered Accountants and a member of the Lesotho Institute of Accountants. I am a current MBA student at the African Leadership University, Rwanda. I previously worked at Pricewaterhouse Coopers and South African Breweries where I gained experience in professional services and financial planning. I have worked in some of the following countries; South Africa, Lesotho, Mozambique and Ireland and have served clients from other geographies remotely.
I have been involved in various leadership, governance and oversight roles (voluntary and paid) including serving as the youngest member of the Council of the University of the Free State and on its Risk and Audit Committee. I am a member of the Association for the advancement of Black Accountants in Southern Africa and served as its first female Chairperson of the Free State Branch.

About Mamoabi Kolobe

I am an Economic Advisor with over ten years of experience in Management, Research and Development. As a Rhodes Scholar with two Postgraduate degrees from Oxford University, I bring a deep understanding of identification and development of business opportunities, and profitable management of projects from inception to completion.
I have served on a number of Organisational Boards as an Economist, and I am a member of various Research Groups and Professional Associations, such as the Black Association of Rhodes Scholars, Oxford Transitional Justice Research, Rhodes Scholars Southern African Forum and the Economic Society of Lesotho.
I am a frequent member and speaker at various development and policy-maker forums and platforms globally, and I consistently teamed up with leaders, economists and policymakers on plans and strategies related to inclusive growth and overall opportunities for development.
